Use this skill to get the television ratings for the Adult Swim Saturday night Toonami television block.

You can ask for a specific night or a summary of the ratings in a year.


**FAQ**

When does the most recent Saturday night ratings update?

Typically 3-5 business days after the television block occurred

What date ranges is television ratings available?

2012-05-26 through 2017-05-27
2018-11-10 through the present

What do I do if I have the ratings for a night that the skill is currently missing?

Please provide us the source of the missing ratings information as feedback by leaving this skill a review.
